Welcome to the Online Bookstore

Manage your books, authors, and customers here.

Use the following endpoints to interact with the bookstore:

  • Get all books: /books
  • Create a book: POST to /books
  • Update a book: PUT to /books/{id}
  • Delete a book: DELETE to /books/{id}
  • Get a book by ID: /books/{id}
  • Search books: GET to /books/search
